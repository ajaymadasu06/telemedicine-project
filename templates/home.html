Our Services

Patient Portal

Patients can submit symptoms and request online consultation with doctors.

Doctor Dashboard

Doctors can view patient details sent from the ambulance and guide paramedics in providing emergency care.

Paramedic Portal

Paramedics can enter the patient tracking ID to view doctor instructions and emergency guidance in real time.
